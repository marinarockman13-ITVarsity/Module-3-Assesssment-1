Dagwood Sandwich

Prep Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Spread small amount of mayonnaise on 8 slices of toast.
  2. Place 1 turkey slice, 1 tomato slice, 1 lettuce leaf,1 Cheese slice, 2 avocado slices​ and 4 half slices of bacon on each of 4 slices of toast with mayonnaise.
  3. Add ​​1 slice of toast with mayonnaise and repeat layer of turkey, tomato, lettuce,cheese, avocado and bacon on each sandwich. Top with remaining slices of toast.
  4. Secure sandwiches with toothpicks and cut in half.
